## ⌨️ **Smart Navigation Examples**

### Navigation Works With:
```powershell

Get-ChildItem           # Alt+Left/Right: Get | Child | Item
New-PSSession          # Alt+Left/Right: New | PS | Session

# Method calls

# Variables with underscores
```
